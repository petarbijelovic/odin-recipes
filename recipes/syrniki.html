Syrniki

Description

Russian Syrniki (сырники) recipe — fluffy, slightly sweet cheese pancakes made from tvorog (farmer's cheese).

They're popular for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 400 g (14 oz) tvorog (or substitute with farmer’s cheese or well-drained ricotta)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cheese:

    If your tvorog is very wet, drain it through a cheesecloth or press lightly. Then mash it with a fork or blend it until smooth.

  2. Mix the batter:

    In a large bowl, combine tvorog, egg, sugar, salt, and vanilla. Stir well.

    Add flour gradually until the dough is sticky but shapeable. Don't overdo the flour — too much makes them tough.

  3. Shape the syrniki:

    Dust your hands and work surface with flour.

    Take a heaping tablespoon of dough and form a small patty (about 1.5-2 cm thick). Lightly coat each side with flour.
